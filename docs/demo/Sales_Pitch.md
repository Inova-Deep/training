## How It Works: A Simple Workflow

### Step 1: Define What Each Role Needs
```
Job Role (e.g., Maintenance Technician)
    ↓
Required Competencies:
    - Permit to Work (PTW)
    - Lock-Out/Tag-Out (LOTO)
    - Working at Height
    - Confined Space Entry
    - Site Safety Induction
```

**You decide once what each role requires.** The system remembers and applies it automatically to everyone in that role.

---

### Step 2: See Everyone's Status at a Glance

| Employee | Role | Safety Cert | First Aid | Induction | Status |
|----------|------|-------------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Gavin T. | Maintenance Tech | Valid | Expiring Soon | Valid | **Authorized** |
| Sarah S. | Maintenance Tech | Expired | Valid | Valid | **Not Authorized** |
| Alex N. | Maintenance Tech | Required | Required | In Progress | **Not Authorized** |

**Color-coded status means instant visibility:**
- **Green (Valid)** - Good to go
- **Yellow (Expiring)** - Needs renewal within 30 days
- **Red (Expired/Required)** - Action needed now

---

### Step 3: Automatic Alerts & Actions

**The system tells you what needs attention:**

> **5 employees** have certifications expiring in the next 30 days
> 
> **2 employees** are not authorized for independent work due to expired safety training
> 
> **3 new hires** need to complete their induction

**No more surprises. No more manual tracking.**

---

### Step 4: Manage Training Until Complete

When a gap is identified, the system creates a **Training Need**:

```
Training Need Created:
  - What: LOTO Recertification
  - Who: Sarah Smith
  - Due By: March 15, 2026
  - Assigned To: Training Coordinator
  - Status: Open -> In Progress -> Completed
```

**Every action is tracked. Every completion is verified.**

---

### Step 5: Upload Evidence & Verify

**Employees can:**

- Add notes and reference numbers

**Managers/Assessors can:**
- Review and approve evidence
- Set expiry dates
- Sign off on observations

## Safety-Critical Features

### Independent Work Authorization

**The system automatically determines who can work independently:**

```
Employee: John Smith
Role: Maintenance Technician

Gating Requirements:
  - LOTO Certification ............ Valid
  - Permit to Work ................ Valid
  - Working at Height ............. Valid
  - Confined Space Entry .......... Valid
  - Site Induction ................ Valid

Result: AUTHORIZED for Independent Work
```

**If ANY gating requirement expires:**
```
  - LOTO Certification ............ EXPIRED

Result: NOT AUTHORIZED
Action Required: Complete recertification before independent work
```

**This protects your people and your organization.**
